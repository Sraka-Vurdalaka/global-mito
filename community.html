Community

Global Mito is the creation of an international expert community that systematizes experience, innovations, and best practices, and through co-creation builds navigation across fields, technologies, products, and experts in the mito-industry.

We bring together leading experts in science, practice, and business. One of the main results of Global MITO is the creation the base for an intercultural expert community (global virtual collaborative culture - GVCC). The principles of GVCC allow us to bring together science, practice, and business, and create a knowledge base that will be at the intersection of these fields and will be of interest to all parties. Each participant becomes a co-author of collective knowledge through exchange, co-creative initiatives, and projects.

We are creating an Expert Map of the mitochondrial health market—a directory of experts (individuals) and their specializations in various areas such as science, production, medicine, cosmetology, etc. This will help identify specialists who can become key contributors to the development of the expert community.
