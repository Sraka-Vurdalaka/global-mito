History

Global Mito is the exciting opportunities for co-creation between the mitochondrial health industry, KMGN, (Global Leaders who Innovate Next Knowledge) and MXR.world (‘mixers across the world’).

KMGN https://www.kmglobalnetwork.org/ Knowledge Management Global Network (KMGN) is a not-for profit network of international KM communities and associations that endeavours to connect KM professionals and facilitate co-creation and sharing of KM assets and resources. Founded in 2014 and institutionalised in 2021, KMGN is supported by a Board of Directors, Functional Committees, and a Co-Leading Team (Chairs).

GLINK (Global Leaders who Innovate Next Knowledge) GLINK is an organisation focused on knowledge management, innovation, and collaborative co-creation of knowledge through events and workshops. It has been bringing together leaders from 30 countries to create innovation since 2017. It cultivates communities that promote the creation, sharing, and management of knowledge, particularly through digital transformation and collaborative efforts. It has hosted a wide range of summits and workshops in multiple countries with engaging themes, eg. GVCC (Global Virtual Collaborative Culture). It has been bringing together leaders from 30 countries to create innovation since 2017.

MXR.world (‘mixers across the world’) https://www.mxr.world MXR.world is a knowledge platform that specialises in innovation conversations. In fast-moving and technologically-converging fields, the MXR methodology involves crisp and well-curated roundtables featuring innovators, industry leaders, and ecosystem enablers. Over the past three years, over 75 events have been held, with 175 pitchdecks of innovators published online for greater business connects: https://www.mxr.world/pitch-decks
